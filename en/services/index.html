Services

Technical diagnostics, technical inspections, certification

The LLC "Scientific and Production Firm "Zond" provides services for expert examination (technical diagnosis), technical reviews (including primary, periodic (regular full and partial), additional), certification of machines, mechanisms, equipment of increased danger in accordance with the Permit of the State Service Mining Supervision and Industrial Safety of Ukraine No. 389.15.30 (valid until May 27, 2020) and is an authorized organization in accordance with the Order of the State Committee for the State Service Mining Supervision № 59 from 15.03.2010.

Expertise of enterprises

The LLC "Scientific and Production Firm "Zond" provides services for conducting an expert examination of an enterprise regarding compliance with the requirements of legislation on occupational safety and industrial safety during the execution of the stated works; During the operation of the declared machines, mechanisms, equipment and their compliance with the requirements of the said legislation; Conducting an examination of the enterprise regarding the conformity of such machines, mechanisms, equipment to the requirements of legislation on occupational safety and industrial safety.

Development of normative documents

The LLC "Scientific and Production Firm "Zond" has considerable experience in developing normative documents of various types - methodologies, technical specifications, standards of organization of Ukraine, national standards of Ukraine and standards harmonized with international and European ones - for the oil and gas and related industries.

Certification tests

Conducting tests to assess the conformity of products in the declared area of activity, designing test results, determining the suitability and conformity of products to regulatory documents, particularly the compliance with the requirements of DSTU ISO / IEC 17025: 2006 according to the Accreditation Certificate No. 2T593 (valid until August 27, 2018).
